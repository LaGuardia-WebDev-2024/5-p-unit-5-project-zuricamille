Info

Bryant Park has to be one of my favorite places to go around christmas time as a New York Resident. It's so pretty around this time of year and has things including;
-Holiday Shops: The market features over 150 boutique-style kiosks in custom-designed glass and metal booths. Vendors offer unique gifts, artisanal goods, holiday decorations, clothing, jewelry, and locally made products. It's a great spot for finding one-of-a-kind presents and supporting small businesses.
-Ice Skating Rink: The rink is free to use (bring your own skates), though skate rentals and locker services are available for a fee. The rink operates alongside the market and is a focal point of the Winter Village.
-Food and Drink: The market has plenty of food stalls offering festive treats, international cuisines, and beverages. Popular items include hot cocoa, mulled wine, pretzels, waffles, and holiday-themed desserts. There is a rink-side lounge and other spots to sit and enjoy the atmosphere.
-Seasonal Entertainment: Throughout the season, the market hosts live performances, holiday-themed events, and activities for kids. Carolers, musicians, and other entertainers add to the holiday spirit.
